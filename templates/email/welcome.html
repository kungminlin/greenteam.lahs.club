Sustainability Projects

We have organized numerous projects on LAHS's campus, including the installation of solar panels and the elimination of single-use plastics. We also host fun events throughout the school year to foster a culture of sustainability on campus. Help us plan these fun projects and events while gaining valuable leadership skills!

 
 

Community Outreach

Through our large social media presence and collaboration with multiple organizations across communities, you will have the opportunity to interact with environmental leaders throughout the Bay Area and beyond. In our annually organized Students for Green High Schools conference, we invite 100+ student leaders from 20+ high schools across the Bay Area to share their environmental initiatives!

Volunteer Opportunities

We offer fun, outdoor volunteer opportunities every week, including habitat restorations, beach cleanups, and even bike repairs! Choose the opportunities you want to participate in and save the environment while also earning volunteer hours!
