On January 20, 2018, Henry M. Gunn Senior High School will host its eighth TEDx conference at Haymarket Theatre in Palo Alto. Hundreds of high schoolers and teachers will join over 10 distinguished speakers from all walks of life sharing Ideas Worth Spreading.

Meet the Team

Emilio Sanchez-Harris

President

Emilio Sanchez-Harris is a senior at Los Altos High School. He’s been an editor for the New Media Literacy journalism class for the past two years. He’s also been a member of the LAHS Marching Band for three years. Next year, he hopes to study business and music management. This is his second year being part of the TEDx team.

Lisa Deng

President

Lisa Deng is a senior at Los Altos High School. She has been dancing for almost seven years and has been part of the LAHS Advanced Jazz Dance class for four years. She is the Community Commissioner of the Mountain View City Youth Advisory Committee and is a member of the MVLA Speech and Debate Team. She intends to major in business or hospitality management next year. This is her second year being part of the TEDx team.

Julia Santos

President of Speaker Management

Julia Santos is a senior at Los Altos High School. She is a Senior Writer and Business Manager for the school newspaper, The Talon, which she has been a part of for three years. She has played Varsity Water Polo for four years, serving as the Captain for three years. Julia is interested in pursuing public policy and political science while she plays water polo for UCSB next year. This is her second year being part of the TEDx team.

Eric Warmoth

President of Promotion

Eric Warmoth is a senior at Los Altos High School. He is a member of the National Honors Society. Eric has played Varsity Water Polo for three years and served as the captain for his senior season. He has been award Scholar Athlete of the Year for the last three years. Eric plans to pursue a major in Marine Science and Ocean Engineering. This is his second year being part of the TEDxLAHS team.

Madison Woo

President of Finance

Madison Woo is a junior at Los Altos High School. She is a Senior Writer for the school newspaper, The Talon. She plays the clarinet at El Camino Youth Symphony and has been playing clarinet for seven years. This is her second year being part of the TEDx team.

Gialon Kasha

Gialon Kasha is a senior at Los Altos High School. He is a defense attorney for his school’s Mock Trial club, a guest writer for the Talon, a tutor for LAHS Teen Tutors, and a piano player. He plans to double major in physics and computer science at Carnegie Mellon University next year. This is his second year being part of the TEDx team.
